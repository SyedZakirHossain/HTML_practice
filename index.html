ACTIVE AND PASSIVE VOICE

Compare:-1. Lipi helps Sumi. 2. Sumi is helped by Lipi. These two above sentences express the same meaning. But in sentence 1, the form of the Verb shows that the person denoted by the subject does something.[the subject “Lipi “ performs the act; Lipi (the person denoted by the subject) does something. The Verb helps is said to be in the Active Voice. In sentence 2, the form of the Verb shows that something is done to the person denoted by the Subject. Something is done to Sumi (the person denoted by the Subject.) The Verb helped is said to be in the Passive Voice.

Shamim writes a letter. A letter is written by Shamin. The first sentence is in the active voice.In it i. the subject ("Shamim")performs the act; ii.there is a transitive verb(writes);and iii.an object("a letter") The second sentence is in the passive voice.In It the object of the first sentence becomes the subject("a letter") ;ii.the object of the first sentence becomes the subject("Shamim") and is governed by the preposition "by"; and iii.the verb takes its past perticiple form ("written") and is preceded by the form of the auxiliary verb to be 9is) in the same tense as the verb in the first sentence and agreeing with the new subject.
